Welcome

Welcome to Washington D.C. Roleplay Staff Team. This handbook contains all essential policies and procedures required for your role. As a staff member, you are expected to maintain a high standard of professionalism and maturity at all times. Adherence to these guidelines is mandatory for all ranks unless a written exemption is provided by a Leadership member. Failure to comply with these policies will result in disciplinary action.
