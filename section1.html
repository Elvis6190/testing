Section I: General Policies & Professional Conduct

1.1 Chain of Command

  • 1.1.1 The server operates on a strict chain of command. You must follow it at all times for questions, issues, and escalations.
  • 1.1.2 Failure to follow the chain of command may result in an infraction.

1.2 Professionalism

  • 1.2.1 You must maintain professionalism at all times when representing the staff team.
  • 1.2.2 Respect: You must respect all community members, staff peers, and superiors. Disrespect towards higher ranks is prohibited.
  • 1.2.3 Drama and Arguing: Causing, starting, or participating in drama is strictly prohibited and will result in severe punishment, such as a demotion and timeout.
